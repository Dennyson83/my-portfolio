About me

I hold a bachelor's degree in Biomedical Sciences and a PhD in Bioinformatics from the University of São Paulo (USP). I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Laboratory of Immunology and Molecular Regulation (EACH-USP). My work focuses on data analysis and visualization for biological and biomedical sciences. I have practical experience in managing FASTQ/BCF pipelines and preprocessing sequencing data in whole exome sequencing (WES) and transcriptomics (Bulk, Single-Cell [BD rhapsody and 10x genomics], and spatial transcriptomics [10x genomics]).

I design reproducible workflows, provide clear visual insights, and apply advanced statistics and experimental design (e.g., G*Power) to support robust study design. I primarily program in R, Python, Julia, and Bash (Linux) and am comfortable working with SSH on remote servers. I also develop software tools and APIs for data analysis. I speak advanced English and have collaborated with national and international teams.

I currently provide services in data analysis, systems biology, and as a back-end developer using generative AI.
